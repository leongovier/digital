# Case Study Content Template

Use this file to draft content before adding it to product.html.
Constraints are strict — they map directly to rendered line counts at card width.

---

## TAB LABEL
**Max 20 characters.**
> e.g. `Cubik CRM`

---

## SUBHEADER
**Format:** `[Project Name] — [one-sentence descriptor]`
**Max 12 words after the dash. No full stop.**
> e.g. `Cubik CRM — A solo-built broker admin suite that fuses AI quoting with pipeline tracking`

---

## SUMMARY
**Max 60 words. 2–3 sentences.**
Describe: what the product does, who uses it, and how it was built/shipped.
Do NOT repeat the subheader. Start with the product name.

> e.g. `Cubik CRM turns broker enquiries into pipeline-tracked deals in a single workspace...`

---

## PROBLEM CARD

**Max 2 sentences. Max 30 words.**
State the context and core friction. No solution language.
