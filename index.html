Working together

How we'll work together.

With nearly thirty years across every part of design, I build websites, calculators, quote and decisioning engines and bespoke CRM systems that genuinely work for your business — and I do it without baffling you with tech. Here's exactly what that looks like in practice.

01 / Discovery & direction

We agree what you actually need

Website, tool, system, or all three — I ask the right questions. For bespoke work this means a proper discovery conversation to understand how you actually operate. We agree scope, a realistic timeline and payment schedule before a line of code is written.

02 / Build & review

Live progress, start to finish

Once designs are agreed, I start building and share a live link from day one. You can review progress, leave notes and sign off deliverables as we go — no waiting until the end to see what you're getting, and no surprises on delivery.

03 / Test & iterate

Right for your customers

As the build progresses we test on real devices, review with fresh eyes and refine as needed. Making sure it works properly for your customers and your team is as important as how it looks — both have to be right before anything goes live.

04 / Launch & analyse

We go live and keep watching

We publish, migrate to your hosting (or I set one up if you need it), and once you're live I don't just walk away. I track performance against the goals we agreed at the start, so we know exactly what's working and what to adjust.
